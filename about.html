Our Mission

Intelligent Coordination When It Matters Most

Disasters are unpredictable and unforgiving. Effective response depends on fast, clear, and coordinated decision-making under extreme pressure. ResQAI exists to put AI-powered disaster intelligence in the hands of emergency managers, first responders, and affected communities — bridging the gap between the moment a disaster strikes and the moment coordinated professional response is fully mobilised.

We believe responsible AI, anchored in safety-first design and grounded in disaster management best practices, can meaningfully reduce harm during crises. Every ResQAI response is shaped by expert-authored AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS that enforce factual accuracy, official-authority deference, and strict disaster-only scope.

Under the Hood

How the AI Works

1. You Describe the Disaster

Type your disaster scenario, select a evacuation situation, fill in an incident form, or click a quick-prompt for your disaster type. Your input is never stored.

2. Granite Applies Safety Rules

Your message is prepended with AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS — a system prompt enforcing disaster-only scope, official-authority deference, factual accuracy, and refusal of medical diagnosis or non-emergency topics — before reaching IBM Granite.

3. Structured Guidance Delivered

The model returns numbered, actionable steps tailored to your disaster type. For reports and evacuation plans, it produces fully-formatted professional documents ready for coordination teams.

Responsible AI
Safety Design Principles
Official Authority First
Every AI response is anchored with a standing instruction to defer to official emergency management authority directives — FEMA, local EOC, national disaster management agencies — above all else.
Disaster-Only Scope
The model is instructed to refuse all non-disaster topics. It will not engage with standalone medical diagnosis, politics, entertainment, or anything outside disaster management. Medical concerns are always referred to EMS.
Low Temperature Inference
The model runs at temperature 0.3 to maximise factual consistency and minimise hallucination — critical in disaster response and rescue coordination contexts where accuracy directly affects safety.
No Speculation on Casualties
ResQAI is explicitly instructed never to speculate on casualty numbers, structural safety, or disaster severity assessments — directing users to official assessments from qualified authorities.

AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS is a constant defined in app.py that is prepended to every prompt sent to Granite. Modifying this constant is the primary way to customise ResQAI's behaviour without changing the model itself.
